Webhook Setup Guide

1

Go to your trading platform and find the webhook/alert settings

2

In the alert form, find the "Webhook URL" field

3

Enter your webhook URL (see below)

{{ request.host_url }}strategy/webhook/YOUR_WEBHOOK_ID

Replace YOUR_WEBHOOK_ID with the webhook ID from your strategy details

Alert Name Keywords

Include one of these keywords in your alert name:

BUY Long entry
SELL Long exit
SHORT Short entry
COVER Short cover

Keywords are not case-sensitive and can be anywhere in the alert name.
